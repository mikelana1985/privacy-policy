Privacy Policy for Newton's Law

Last updated: April 2025

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how our game "Newton's Law" ("we", "us", or "our") handles user data and advertising.

2. Data Collection and Use

Our game uses Google AdMob for advertising. Through AdMob, the following data may be collected:

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • Advertising ID
  • Game usage data
  • Approximate location (if permitted by device settings)

3. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display three types of advertisements in our game:

  • Banner ads (ca-app-pub-9601818723972579/9317127983) during gameplay
  • Interstitial ads (ca-app-pub-9601818723972579/7830426548) after game over
  • Rewarded video ads (ca-app-pub-9601818723972579/1599184406) in the pause menu

AdMob may use cookies and device identifiers to deliver personalized ads.

4. Children's Privacy

Our game does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. All ads are family-friendly and age-appropriate.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  • Purchase the premium version to remove ads entirely
